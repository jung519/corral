Corral

Ship issues while you sleep — with a human at every gate.

Corral takes an issue from your tracker, has an AI agent plan it, waits for your approval, implements it, self-reviews, and opens a pull request. Runs on your own machine, with your own keys.

Download View on GitHub

Early and unsigned — macOS: right-click → Open; Windows: More info → Run anyway. No installers yet? Build from source.

Tracker → AI plan → Your approval → Code → Self-review → Pull request

BYOK

Your keys, in your OS keychain. The app embeds nothing and phones nothing home.

Provider-neutral

Claude, Gemini, or GPT — via their CLI or API. Mix per stage: plan, build, review.

Human in the loop

You approve the plan and the review. Nothing merges without you.

Get started

Download the app above (or build from source), then the built-in setup wizard walks you through connecting your provider, tracker, and repository — no config files to edit. Then pick an issue and approve the plan.
